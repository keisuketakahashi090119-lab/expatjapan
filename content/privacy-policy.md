You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

We use **Google AdSense** to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies to show ads based on your browsing history. You can manage ad preferences at [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/).

Affiliate links and embedded content (e.g., from italki, Wise, Rakuten) may set their own cookies. We are not responsible for third-party cookie policies.
