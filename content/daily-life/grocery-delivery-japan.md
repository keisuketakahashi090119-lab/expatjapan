Grocery delivery in Japan has exploded since 2020. Whether you're too busy to shop, can't carry heavy bags, or just hate navigating Japanese supermarkets alone, these services are a genuine time-saver.

---

## Best Grocery Delivery Services in Japan

### 1. Amazon Fresh — Best for Convenience

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| Coverage | Tokyo, Kanagawa, Chiba, Osaka, other major areas |
| Minimum order | No minimum for Prime members |
| Delivery time | 2-hour windows, same day often available |
| App language | English (use amazon.co.jp) |
| Price level | Competitive, similar to in-store |

If you already have **Amazon Prime Japan** (¥600/month), Amazon Fresh is the easiest option. The website works in English, and you can recognize product photos even without reading Japanese.

**Amazon Prime Japan:** [amazon.co.jp/prime](https://www.amazon.co.jp/prime)

---

### 2. Oisix (オイシックス) — Best for Quality & Organic

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| Coverage | Nationwide (most areas) |
| Specialty | Organic vegetables, high-quality produce |
| Delivery | Weekly set boxes or individual items |
| App language | Japanese only |
| Price level | Premium (expect to pay 20–30% more than supermarket) |

Oisix is popular with health-conscious families. Their **Kit Oisix** meal kits are excellent — pre-portioned ingredients with recipe cards. Some English recipe content available.

**[Oisix](https://www.oisix.com/)** — try their trial set first

---

### 3. Seiyu (西友) / Walmart — Best for Budget Shoppers

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| Coverage | Greater Tokyo and Osaka areas |
| Specialty | Regular supermarket items at low prices |
| Delivery | Next-day or same-day in some areas |
| App language | Japanese only |
| Price level | Budget-friendly |

Seiyu's online store is great for everyday staples — rice, noodles, condiments, cleaning products. Prices are close to in-store.

---

### 4. Co-op (コープ) — Best for Families

Consumer cooperative with weekly delivery routes. You join as a member and get weekly deliveries.

- **Weekly catalog** system — order by Tuesday, delivered Friday
- Family-focused product selection
- Baby food, organic options, household items
- Not all areas covered

Check coverage: [生協 (co-op) locator](https://jccu.coop/)

---

### 5. Life (ライフ) / Tokyu Store Online
Major supermarket chains with online ordering. Good selection if there's a branch in your area.

## Quick Grocery Delivery (30–60 min)

For urgent needs:

### Uber Eats Grocery
- Convenience store items delivered in 30–45 minutes
- More expensive than shopping yourself, but convenient for forgotten items
- Available in most major cities

### Ōkubo Stores (Near you)
Many local stores and supermarkets partner with **Wolt** or **menu** for fast delivery.

## Tips for Ordering in Japanese

Even with limited Japanese, you can order groceries:

1. **Use Google Translate camera** — Point at product labels to understand contents
2. **Filter by category** — Most apps have category icons (野菜 = vegetables, 肉 = meat, 魚 = fish)
3. **Search in English** — Amazon Fresh accepts English search terms like "milk," "cheese," "chicken"
4. **Save your favorites** — Once you find what you like, reordering is easy without reading Japanese

## International Foods in Japan

Finding international ingredients:
- **[iHerb](https://www.iherb.com/)** — Ships supplements, Western health foods to Japan
- **Costco Japan** — Bulk Western products; membership required (¥4,840/year)
- **カルディ (KALDI)** — International food store chain with imported goods
- **Meidi-ya (明治屋)** — Specialty imported food stores
- **Don Quijote (ドン・キホーテ)** — Surprisingly good imported food section
