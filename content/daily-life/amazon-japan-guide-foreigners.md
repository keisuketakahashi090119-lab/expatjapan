Amazon Japan (Amazon.co.jp) is one of the most useful tools for foreigners in Japan — it delivers almost anything the next day, accepts foreign credit cards, and has a surprisingly usable English interface. Here's everything you need to know to use it effectively.

---

## Setting Up Amazon Japan as a Foreigner

### Can I Use My Existing Amazon Account?

**Yes — but with conditions.** Amazon operates separate regional accounts. Your amazon.com (US) account is different from amazon.co.jp. You can:
- Create a new amazon.co.jp account with your existing email
- Or use the "Amazon Global" feature on amazon.com for some Japan-shipping items

**Recommendation:** Create a dedicated amazon.co.jp account for the full Japanese catalog and faster delivery options.

### Creating an Amazon Japan Account

1. Visit [amazon.co.jp](https://www.amazon.co.jp)
2. Click アカウント (Account) → 新規登録 (Register new account)
3. Enter your email address, name, and password
4. Verify your email
5. Add a Japanese address as your default delivery address

**Using the site in English:** Amazon.co.jp doesn't have a full English mode, but you can:
- Use Chrome's auto-translate (right-click → Translate to English) — works well for most pages
- Use the Amazon Japan app with translation support
- Many product pages have English descriptions if the item is internationally available

## Amazon Prime Japan

### Cost (2025)

- **Monthly:** ¥600/month
- **Annual:** ¥5,900/year (saves ¥1,300 vs monthly)

### What's Included

**Shopping benefits:**
- Free same-day or next-day delivery on millions of items
- Free shipping on orders (standard members pay ¥450+ per order without Prime)
- Priority access to Lightning Deals

**Streaming (Prime Video Japan):**
- Extensive Japanese content (anime, J-drama, variety shows)
- International content library (somewhat different from US/UK)
- Amazon Originals in Japanese

**Other benefits:**
- Prime Music (limited catalog)
- Prime Reading (Japanese books/manga)
- Amazon Photos (unlimited photo storage)

### Prime Video: Japan vs US Comparison

Prime Video Japan has significant Japanese-language content not available on other markets, making it popular among foreigners learning Japanese or interested in Japanese media. International content (Hollywood, UK shows) is available but may differ from US catalog. A VPN can sometimes access other regions' catalogs.

## Delivery Speed in Japan

Amazon Japan's delivery speed is genuinely impressive:

| Service | Speed | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Same-day delivery (当日お急ぎ便) | Order by noon, arrive today | ¥514 (free with Prime) |
| Next-day delivery (お急ぎ便) | 1 day | ¥396 (free with Prime) |

Same-day delivery is available in most major cities (Tokyo, Osaka, Nagoya, Fukuoka). In rural areas, next-day is more typical.

## What to Buy on Amazon Japan

Amazon Japan is particularly good for:
- **Electronics** — Japanese market electronics, imported gadgets
- **Books and manga** — vast selection of Japanese-language content
- **Household goods** — cheaper than Japanese retail stores for many items
- **Food** — pantry staples, foreign food brands, health foods
- **Daily consumables** — laundry, cleaning, personal care products (Subscribe & Save available)
- **Furniture assembly** — order from Amazon, get assembly service added

**Price comparison tip:** Amazon Japan isn't always cheapest. Check Rakuten Ichiba and Yahoo! Shopping for comparison — cashback programs (Rakuten Points, PayPay) can make them cheaper for some items.

## International Shipping from Amazon Japan

Some Amazon Japan items can be shipped internationally — useful for sending gifts or moving. Check the product page for "国際配送" (international shipping) availability.

**Limitations:**
- Not all sellers ship internationally (third-party sellers especially)
- Amazon Japan's own inventory ships more reliably internationally
- Customs duties and taxes apply in the destination country

**For foreigners leaving Japan:** If you're returning home and want to ship purchases you made in Japan, AmazonGlobal or international courier services (Kuroneko Yamato International, Japan Post EMS) are options.

## Amazon Japan Tips and Tricks

1. **Subscribe & Save (定期おトク便):** 5% off repeat purchases of consumables. Set up for toilet paper, laundry detergent, and daily items.

2. **Lightning Deals (タイムセール):** Timed offers with countdown clocks. Check daily in the morning.

3. **Amazon Sale Events:** Prime Day (July), Black Friday (November), and New Year Sale (January) are the biggest. Many electronics and appliances drop 20–40%.

4. **Warehouse Deals (Amazon Warehouse):** Open-box and returned items at significant discounts. Often like-new condition with full return protection.

5. **Family plan:** Amazon Prime Family offers additional 15% off diapers/baby products if you have children. Register your child's birthday in account settings.

6. **Amazon locker (宅配ボックス):** If your apartment doesn't have a delivery box, use Amazon Locker at convenience stores for secure pickup.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I use Amazon Japan with a foreign credit card?**
Yes — foreign Visa and Mastercard cards generally work on Amazon.co.jp. Add your card in the payment settings and verify it accepts charges from Japan.

**Is Amazon Prime Japan worth it for foreigners?**
Yes, for most foreigners in Japan. At ¥5,900/year (~¥490/month), the free delivery alone pays for itself quickly. The streaming library (Prime Video Japan) adds significant value, especially for access to anime and Japanese drama.

**Can I use my Amazon US account on Amazon Japan?**
No — Amazon Japan is a separate regional platform. You need to create a separate amazon.co.jp account. However, you can use the same email address.

**Is Amazon Japan in English?**
Not officially, but Chrome's auto-translate makes the site largely functional in English. Many product pages also have English descriptions for internationally sold products.
