Japan's **Highly Skilled Professional (高度専門職) visa** is the fastest path to permanent residency in Japan. If you qualify, you can get PR in **1–3 years** instead of the standard 10 years.

---

## What Is the HSP Visa?

The Highly Skilled Professional (HSP) visa is a points-based visa designed to attract top foreign talent to Japan.

**Three categories:**
- **HSP Type 1-i:** Advanced academic research activities
- **HSP Type 1-ii:** Advanced specialized/technical activities (most common — IT, engineering, finance)
- **HSP Type 1-iii:** Advanced business management activities

**If you score 70+ points → HSP visa eligible**  
**If you score 80+ points → PR in 1 year**  
**If you score 70–79 points → PR in 3 years**

---

## How the Points System Works

Points are accumulated from multiple factors:

### Academic Background

| Education | Points |
|---|---|
| Doctoral degree | 30 |
| Master's degree | 20 |
| Bachelor's degree | 10 |
| Professional degree (MBA, JD, etc.) | 5 (in addition to above) |

### Work Experience

| Years of Experience | Points |
|---|---|
| 10+ years | 20 |
| 7–9 years | 15 |
| 5–6 years | 10 |
| 3–4 years | 5 |

### Annual Salary

| Salary | Points |
|---|---|
| ¥10M+ | 40 |
| ¥9M–10M | 35 |
| ¥8M–9M | 30 |
| ¥7M–8M | 25 |
| ¥6M–7M | 20 |
| ¥5M–6M | 15 |
| ¥4M–5M | 10 |
| ¥3M–4M | 5 |

### Bonus Points

| Bonus Criteria | Points |
|---|---|
| Work for designated innovation company | +10 |
| JLPT N1 or BJT J1+ | +15 |
| JLPT N2 or BJT J1 | +10 |
| Graduated from Japanese university | +10 |
| R&D investment by company over 3% | +5 |
| Japanese government-designated innovation hub | +10 |
| Age under 30 | +15 |
| Age 30–34 | +10 |
| Age 35–39 | +5 |

## Benefits of HSP Visa

Beyond fast-track PR, HSP holders get:

| Benefit | Details |
|---|---|
| **Multiple activities permitted** | Can do side work and consulting |
| **Spouse can work** | Spouse gets permission to work without restrictions |
| **Parents may stay** | Under certain conditions, parents can reside in Japan |
| **Live-in domestic helper** | Can employ foreign domestic helper |
| **5-year residence period** | Longest standard residence period |
| **Preferential processing** | Faster immigration processing |

## HSP Type 1-ii (Most Common for Foreign Engineers/Professionals)

This is the category most relevant for:
- Software engineers
- IT professionals
- Finance/banking professionals
- Researchers
- Marketing/business professionals

**Requirements:**
- Score 70+ on the points system
- Have a job offer (or current employment) matching the HSP activities
- Salary must match your point calculation

## Application Process

### Step 1 — Calculate Your Points
Use the calculator above. Be conservative — you'll need to prove everything with documents.

### Step 2 — Gather Documents
- Points calculation declaration form
- Diploma(s) with certified translation
- Employment certificate
- Salary slips or tax returns
- JLPT certificate (if applicable)
- Passport, residence card

### Step 3 — Apply at Immigration Bureau
Current residents: Apply at your regional Immigration Services Agency.  
Applying from abroad: Your employer's HR typically handles Certificate of Eligibility application.

### Step 4 — Receive HSP Designation
Once approved, your residence card will show 高度専門職 (Highly Skilled Professional).

## Path to Permanent Residency

With HSP visa:

| Score | PR Eligibility |
|---|---|
| 80+ points | After **1 year** of HSP status |
| 70+ points | After **3 years** of HSP status |

Compare to standard PR requirement: **10 years** of continuous residence (or 5 years on work visa with good record).

This is the single biggest advantage of the HSP visa. See our [Permanent Residency Guide](/visa/how-to-get-permanent-residency-japan/) for full PR application details.

---

## Tips to Boost Your Score

1. **Get your JLPT N2 or N1** — Worth 10–15 points, potentially the difference between 70 and 85+
2. **Negotiate a higher salary** — Each salary bracket jump adds 5–15 points
3. **Pursue an advanced degree** — An MBA adds 5 extra points on top of the degree category
4. **Join an innovation company** — Certain government-designated companies add +10 points
