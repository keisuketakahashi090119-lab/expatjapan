Revolut is one of the most popular fintech apps in the world — but Japan is a complicated market for it. Here's the honest picture of what Revolut can and can't do for foreigners in Japan.

---

## Does Revolut Work in Japan?

**Short answer: Yes, but with limitations.**

Revolut operates in Japan under a license from the Japan Financial Services Agency (FSA), but the Japanese version has fewer features than Revolut in Europe or the US. Key differences:

- ✅ Multi-currency accounts
- ✅ International money transfers
- ✅ Revolut Mastercard debit card (physical and virtual)
- ✅ Currency exchange at interbank rates (with limits)
- ❌ No Japanese yen IBAN for receiving foreign payments
- ❌ Cryptocurrency trading not available in Japan
- ❌ Some premium features restricted

---

## Setting Up Revolut in Japan

### Step 1 — Download the App

Download Revolut from the App Store or Google Play. Make sure you select Japan as your country.

### Step 2 — Verify Your Identity

You'll need:
- **Passport or residence card (在留カード)**
- Selfie photo for identity verification
- Japanese address

Verification typically takes **1–3 business days**.

### Step 3 — Fund Your Account

You can fund your Revolut account by:
- Linking a Japanese bank account (wire transfer)
- Using a Japanese credit or debit card

### Step 4 — Order Your Card

Request a Revolut card (physical Mastercard). Delivery takes 7–14 business days in Japan.

## Revolut Fees in Japan (2025)

| Plan | Monthly Cost | Exchange Fee | ATM Withdrawals |
|---|---|---|---|
| Standard (Free) | ¥0 | 0.5–1% on weekends | ¥20,000/month free, then 2% |
| Plus | ~¥880/month | 0% up to limit | ¥40,000/month free |
| Premium | ~¥1,480/month | 0% up to limit | ¥60,000/month free |
| Metal | ~¥2,780/month | 0% up to limit | ¥80,000/month free |

**Weekend surcharge:** Revolut charges an extra 0.5–1% for currency exchange on weekends when interbank markets are closed. Plan your exchanges for weekdays.

## What Revolut Is Best For in Japan

### 1. Travel Within Asia

If you travel frequently to other Asian countries (Korea, Taiwan, Thailand, etc.), Revolut is excellent for converting yen to local currencies at near-market rates without visiting an exchange bureau.

### 2. Receiving Foreign Currency Payments

Freelancers or remote workers who receive USD, EUR, or GBP payments can hold those currencies in Revolut and convert when the rate is favorable.

### 3. Budgeting and Spending Tracking

Revolut's app has excellent budget tracking tools — categorized spending, instant notifications, and spending analytics.

### 4. Virtual Cards for Online Shopping

Create disposable virtual cards for one-time online purchases. Useful for international shopping where you don't want to expose your main card number.

## Revolut Limitations to Know

**No Japanese bank account substitute:** Revolut in Japan cannot replace a Japanese bank account. You still need a Japanese account to receive salary, pay rent via bank transfer, or use automatic bill payment (口座振替).

**Weekend exchange surcharges:** The weekend fee can add up if you exchange frequently. Plan exchanges for weekday business hours.

**Transfer limits:** Standard plan users have monthly limits on fee-free exchanges. Heavy users should upgrade to Plus or Premium.

**Customer support:** Support is app-based only (chat). There are no phone lines or physical branches — this can be frustrating for complex issues.

## How to Get the Most Out of Revolut in Japan

1. **Exchange currency on weekdays** to avoid the weekend surcharge
2. **Use the Standard plan first** — upgrade only if you hit the limits
3. **Enable instant payment notifications** to monitor every transaction
4. **Set up a savings vault** to automatically move a percentage of income to savings
5. **Combine with Wise** for large international transfers where mid-market rate matters most
