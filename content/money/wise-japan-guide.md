Wise is one of the best tools available for foreigners managing money in Japan — low fees, real exchange rates, and multi-currency accounts that work across borders. But it has specific limitations in the Japanese market that are worth knowing before you rely on it. Here's the full picture.

---

## What Is Wise?

Wise is an online financial service that lets you:
1. **Send money internationally** at the real exchange rate (not the bank's inflated rate)
2. **Hold money in 40+ currencies** in one account
3. **Receive payments** in foreign currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, etc.) with local bank details
4. **Spend globally** with the Wise debit card (Mastercard)

---

## Setting Up Wise in Japan

### Step 1 — Create Your Account
1. Go to [wise.com](https://wise.com/) or download the app
2. Register with your email address
3. Verify your identity: passport + residence card photo required

This typically takes **24–48 hours** for verification.

### Step 2 — Add a Payment Method
Link your Japanese bank account or credit card to fund transfers and deposits.

### Step 3 — Open Your Wise Account Balances
Once verified, you can hold JPY (yen), USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, and 40+ other currencies.

---

## Sending Money from Japan with Wise

### How It Works

1. Enter how much you want to send (e.g., ¥300,000 to USD)
2. See the real exchange rate and exact fee upfront
3. Transfer yen to Wise's Japanese bank account (SBI Bank account details provided)
4. Wise converts and sends to your recipient

### Cost Example (¥300,000 → USD, April 2025)

| Service | Amount Received |
|---|---|
| Wise | ~$1,975 |
| MUFG Bank wire | ~$1,910 |
| Japan Post | ~$1,935 |

**Wise saves approximately ¥8,000–15,000 per ¥300,000 transfer.**

---

## Receiving Money in Japan with Wise

If you work remotely or receive income from abroad:

### Get Local Bank Details for Foreign Currency

Wise gives you:
- **USD account details** (for US clients, Upwork, etc.)
- **EUR account details** (for European clients)
- **GBP account details** (UK)
- **AUD, CAD, SGD, NZD, and more**

You give your US/EU/UK clients Wise's local bank details. They make a domestic transfer. Wise holds the funds in that currency — no international wire fee for them.

### Convert to Yen When Ready

Once funds are in your Wise account, convert to JPY and withdraw to your Japanese bank at the real rate.

This is the cheapest way for freelancers and remote workers to receive foreign income while living in Japan.

## The Wise Debit Card

Wise offers a **Mastercard debit card** connected to your Wise account.

**Features:**
- Spend in any currency at real mid-market rates
- First ¥30,000/month in foreign currency ATM withdrawals free (fee applies after)
- Works anywhere Mastercard is accepted
- Perfect for travel — no surprise foreign transaction fees

**Apply for the Wise card:** Available through the app after account verification.

## Common Use Cases for Foreigners in Japan

### Sending Money Home Monthly
Set up regular transfers. The rate stays competitive, and you can schedule them automatically.

### Paying International Bills
Foreign streaming services, subscriptions, or family expenses back home — pay with the Wise card at real rates.

### Remote Work Payments
Receive client payments from the US, EU, or UK without your Japanese bank account's SWIFT wire fees.

### Moving to Japan
Bring money over from your home country cheaply to cover initial costs (apartment deposit, appliances, moving fees).

## Is Wise Available for All Nationalities?

Wise is available to residents of Japan regardless of nationality. You need:
- Valid passport
- Japanese residence card (在留カード)
- Japanese address

**Exception:** US citizens/permanent residents face FATCA compliance complexity — Wise may request additional documentation.
