Rakuten's ecosystem of apps and services can save foreigners in Japan significant money — if you understand how the points system actually works. Most people scratch the surface and leave thousands of points unclaimed. Here's how to use it properly.

---

## The Rakuten Ecosystem Explained

Rakuten offers interconnected services where using multiple products **multiplies** the points you earn:

| Service | Benefit |
|---|---|
| Rakuten Card | 1% cashback (1pt per ¥100) on everything |
| Rakuten Bank | Higher interest rate (0.1%) when linked to Card |
| Rakuten Mobile | Discount + points boost |
| Rakuten Market (楽天市場) | 1–15%+ points on purchases |
| Rakuten Securities | Monthly investment earns points |
| Rakuten Pay | Mobile payments with points |
| Rakuten Travel | Points on hotel bookings |

**Rakuten points (楽天ポイント)** are worth ¥1 each and usable across all services.

---

## The Essential Setup for Foreigners

### Step 1 — Rakuten Card (楽天カード) ★ Start Here

Japan's most popular credit card. No annual fee.

- **1% cashback** on all purchases (doubled on Rakuten services)
- **5,000 points** welcome bonus for new applicants
- Accepted almost everywhere Visa is accepted
- Available to foreign residents with residence card

**Apply:** [rakuten-card.co.jp](https://www.rakuten-card.co.jp/)

Requirements:
- Valid residence card
- Income source (employment or self-employment)
- Japanese address

> Foreigners are accepted, though approval depends on credit history in Japan. Having a bank account and been in Japan at least 6 months helps.

---

### Step 2 — Rakuten Bank (楽天銀行)

Japan's largest online bank. Open an account and link it to your Rakuten Card.

Benefits when linked to Rakuten Card:
- Higher interest rate (0.1% vs 0.001% at megabanks)
- Monthly ATM fee waivers (based on activity level)
- Points for automatic bill payments

**Open online:** [rakuten-bank.co.jp](https://www.rakuten-bank.co.jp/)

---

### Step 3 — Rakuten Market Shopping

Shop at [楽天市場](https://www.rakuten.co.jp/) for everyday purchases:

- Base: 1% points
- Add Rakuten Card: +2%
- During SPU promotions: up to 15%+ total

**SPU (Super Point Up Program)** — The key to maximizing points:
- Use Rakuten Card → +2%
- Have Rakuten Mobile → +4%
- Use Rakuten Bank → +1%
- Use Rakuten Pay → +1%
- Have Rakuten Securities → +1%
- And more...

Stack enough services → earn **10–16%** points on Market purchases.

---

### Step 4 — Rakuten Mobile (楽天モバイル)

- ¥3,278/month unlimited data
- +4% points boost on Rakuten Market
- Free calls to other Rakuten Mobile users

For heavy data users, the value + points boost makes this competitive. For light users, MVNO SIMs like IIJmio are cheaper overall.

---

### Step 5 — Rakuten Securities (楽天証券)

For the SPU boost:
- Set up monthly investment (tsumitate) of ¥1,000+ → +1% SPU on Market
- Best combined with NISA account (see [NISA guide](/money/nisa-investing-japan-foreigner/))

---

## Rakuten Pay (楽天ペイ)

Mobile payment app linked to your Rakuten Card:
- Earn points on every payment
- Accepted at major convenience stores, restaurants, supermarkets
- Works with QR code payment (same as PayPay)

---

## Rakuten Travel (楽天トラベル)

Book hotels and flights in Japan through Rakuten Travel to earn points:
- Usually 1–2% base + card bonus
- Special point events periodically offer 5–10%+
- [travel.rakuten.co.jp](https://travel.rakuten.co.jp/)

---

## Point Shopping Events to Know

| Event | When | Bonus |
|---|---|---|
| お買い物マラソン | Monthly | Points per store visited |
| スーパーSALE | Quarterly | Up to 10× points on items |
| 5と0のつく日 | 5th, 10th, 15th, 20th, 25th, 30th | +2% points (with Card) |

Plan major purchases around these events for maximum returns.

## Realistic Savings Example

Monthly spending ¥200,000 through Rakuten stack:
- Rakuten Market: ¥80,000 × 10% = 8,000 points
- Regular Card purchases: ¥120,000 × 1% = 1,200 points
- **Total: ~9,200 points/month = ¥110,400/year**

Heavy Rakuten Market users with all services stacked regularly report ¥100,000–200,000 worth of points per year.
