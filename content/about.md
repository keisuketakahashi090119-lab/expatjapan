## What Is Expat Japan?

**Expat Japan** is a practical English-language guide for foreigners living in — or planning to move to — Japan.

We cover everything you actually need to know: visas, jobs, banking, health insurance, daily life, and more. No fluff, no recycled tourist content — just clear, up-to-date information written for people building a real life in Japan.

---

## Who Is This For?

- **People planning to move to Japan** — and overwhelmed by contradictory information online
- **New arrivals** getting set up: bank accounts, SIM cards, health insurance, apartment hunting
- **Long-term residents** navigating more complex topics: permanent residency, investing, taxes
- **Working holiday makers, students, and professionals** at every stage of expat life

## Our Approach

Information about Japan for foreigners is often outdated, vague, or hard to find in one place. We try to fix that by:

- Writing in plain English (no bureaucratic language)
- Keeping articles up to date with current rules and costs
- Linking to official sources so you can verify things yourself
- Being upfront about what we don't know
