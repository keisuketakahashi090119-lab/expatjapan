### Post #2 — 在日外国人の「見えないコスト」

**グループ:** Living in Japan / Japan Expat Community  
**タイミング:** 月末（家賃・請求書タイミング）

---

Nobody warned me how expensive the first year in Japan actually is. 😅

It's not just rent. It's:
- Agency fee (仲介手数料): 1 month's rent
- Key money (礼金): 0–2 months
- Deposit (敷金): 1–2 months
- Guarantor fee: ~10,000¥/yr
- Moving company
- Initial furniture/appliances
- Setting up utilities

I ended up needing ¥600,000+ before I even started paying normal rent.

Anyone else got sticker shock their first year? What did you wish you'd budgeted for?

### Post #5 — student visa質問（よくある質問に回答する形式）

**グループ:** Moving to Japan / Teaching English in Japan  
**タイミング:** 学期前（8〜9月、1〜2月）

---

Seeing a lot of questions about Japan student visas lately, so here's a quick summary of what I learned:

🎓 **Japan Student Visa basics:**
- Apply at your nearest Japanese embassy/consulate
- Your school handles the CoE (Certificate of Eligibility) — takes 1-3 months
- You need: enrollment letter, financial proof (~¥2M+ in savings or sponsor docs), passport, photos
- Processing after CoE: usually 2-4 weeks
- Works for: language schools, universities, vocational schools

Common mistakes:
❌ Applying too late (start 3-4 months before enrollment)
❌ Not having enough funds documented
❌ Missing the CoE vs visa distinction

Feel free to ask specific questions below — happy to share what I know!
